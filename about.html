About Me

My name is Louis, I’m a traveller from Belgium currently based in Byron Bay.

What I like most about videography is meeting and collaborating with passionate people. I enjoy creating authentic and visually engaging content, telling stories that highlight people, places, and experiences.

I hold a Master’s degree in Communications and have over four years of experience as a Communications Officer in Brussels. This background gives me a strategic approach to video production. Whether it’s for social media, a website, or a promotional project, I take the time to discuss and shape each project carefully. Ensuring it fits into your communication and marketing strategy.
